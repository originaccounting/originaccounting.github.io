我们以真实的公司案例为框架，整理出一套自己的学习材料，通过老师们的讲解，能让同学们学到真正的daily job以及每个职位不同的责任和任务，而这些都是课堂上学不到，需要真正靠时间来积累的经验。我们在培训中会手把手带同学们完成模拟真实情景下的各类事务，让大家掌握真实的会计工作中的流程和步骤。当同学们完成我们的课程的时候，已经可以游刃有余的面对面试和工作了。

所有课程都是我们老师结合自己多年的工作经验开发出来的独家课程，不仅把自己的看家本领拿出来和大家分享，更是结合了时下的办公室文化，将一些在办公中遇到的问题一一道明，为同学们扫清求职路上的障碍，在最短的时间里找到心仪的工作，开始崭新的人生道路。

启源金牌行业会计入职课程：

启源金牌行业会计课程，想入职local大公司accounting team同学们的首选。适合刚刚毕业想找第一份工作的同学, 以及在小公司或者基础职位积累过一定经验的同学。课程里详细介绍了企业里初中级会计职位的相关工作技巧及实际经验，对刚毕业想找第一份工作的同学有莫大的帮助。完成我们的课程的时候可以说同学们已经有资格成为一名合格的会计从业者了

Session 1

Australian Accounting Market Overview, Positions and Duties, Career Development, Recruitment Process, Seek Advertisement, Software Introduction.

Session 2

Accounts Receivable, Daily Bank Reconciliation, Debt Collection Process, AR Aging Analysis, Credit Note, Credit Control, Invoice Disputes, Assist Cash Inflow Projection, Monthly AR Analysis.

Session 3

Accounts Payable, Payment Run Process, Employees’ Expense Reimbursement, AP Bank Reconciliation, AP Month-End Accrual, AP Month-End Adjusting Journal Posting, Bank Reconciliation, Foreign Exchange Payments, Assist Cash Outflow Projection.

Session 4

Payroll, Payroll Legislation, End-to-End Payroll Process, Superannuation Management, Leave Entitlement Management, New Starters, Deductions and Allowances.

Session 5

Senior Payroll, Concessional Super Cap, Employment Termination Payment, Payroll Tax Lodgment and Annual Reconciliation, EOFY Process, General Ledger Reconciliation.

Session 6

Assistant Accountant 1, Cash Flow Projection, Budgeting and Forecasting, Fixed Asset Management, Bank Reconciliation, General Ledger Mapping.

Session 7

Assistant Accountant 2, Business Activity Statement, FBT, Corporate Tax, Payroll Tax, Month-End Closing, Balance Sheet Reconciliation, General Ledger Maintenance, P & L Analysis, Expenses Analysis.

Session 8

Excel Skills, Shortcuts, Conditional Formatting, Worksheet Techniques, Formulas.

Session 9

Vlookup functions, Financial Functions, Complex Formulas, PivotTables.

Session 10

Interview, “Match Principle”, Telephone Interview Skills, Face-to-Face Interview Skills, Company/Interviewer Research, Technical Questions & Answers, Soft Questions & Answers.

Session 11

Resume, Resume Structure, Key Skills, Working History, Achievements, Cover Letter.
